Saucy Pasta

Description

This is a simple one pot pasta dish. There is not any need to precook the noodles. It is a great weekday fast mea after a long day of work or school

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion chopped
  • 3 1/2 cup of water
  • 3 cups dried rotini pasta
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup shredded mozzerlla
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef and onion in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease.
    2. Add water, pasta, and spaghetti sauce to the skillet and bring to a boil, about 5 minutes. Cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes.
    3. Add bell peppers and mushrooms to the skillet and cook for 5 minutes.
    4. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, season with salt and pepper, and serve.
